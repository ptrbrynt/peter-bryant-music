Hi, I'm Peter 👋

I've been singing for as long as I can remember. When I was 11 years old, I was introduced to the weird and wonderful world of **barbershop harmony**, and my life was forever changed.

Since then I've had some pretty incredible experiences.

As a **quartet singer**, I sing with British Champion Quartet [**Limelight**](https://limelightquartet.co.uk), as well as **Happy Sundays**. I've previously sung with other award-winning quartets including **4 On Demand** and **Apollo II**.

I currently direct [**Main Street Sound**](https://mainstreetsound.co.uk) ladies' barbershop chorus, and sing with [**York City Harmonisers**](https://yorkcityharmonisers.com) — a mixed-voice choir I set up with my younger brother, Nick. Previously, I've sung with **Spirit of Harmony** and **Hallmark of Harmony**, winning a few [BABS](https://singbarbershop.com) medals along the way.

I served in various roles on the [**BABS Board**](https://singbarbershop.com) from 2019-2025, and I still present their monthly [**Tune In**](https://singbarbershop.com/podcast) podcast.

I love working with quartets and choruses as a coach/clinician — please do [email me](mailto:hello@peterbryantmusic.com) if you want to arrange something.
